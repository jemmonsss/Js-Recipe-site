prep_time: 10
cook_time: 25
servings: 4
difficulty: Easy
image: /assets/img/tomato-soup.jpg
author: Your Name
date: 2026-07-15
ingredients:
  - 800g canned tomatoes
  - 1 onion
  - 2 cloves garlic
  - 10 basil leaves
  - 200ml cream
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/2 tsp salt
steps:
  - Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  - Add tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  - Blend with basil until smooth.
  - Stir in cream and season with salt.
  - Serve warm with extra basil.
---

Swap the cream for coconut milk to make it dairy-free.
