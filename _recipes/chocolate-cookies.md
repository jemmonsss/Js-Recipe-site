ingredients:
  - 250g butter
  - 200g brown sugar
  - 100g white sugar
  - 2 eggs
  - 350g flour
  - 200g chocolate chips
  - 1 tsp vanilla
  - 1/2 tsp salt
steps:
  - Cream butter and sugars until fluffy.
  - Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  - Fold in flour, salt, and chocolate chips.
  - Scoop onto trays and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 12 minutes.
  - Cool on the tray before eating.
---

For thicker cookies, chill the dough for 30 minutes before baking.
