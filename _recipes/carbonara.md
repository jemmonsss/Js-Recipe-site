ingredients:
  - 400g spaghetti
  - 200g pancetta
  - 4 eggs
  - 100g parmesan
  - 1 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp salt
steps:
  - Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  - Fry the pancetta until crisp.
  - Whisk eggs with grated parmesan and pepper.
  - Toss hot pasta with pancetta, then stir in the egg mixture off the heat.
  - Serve topped with extra parmesan.
---

Carbonara is best made quickly — the heat from the pasta cooks the eggs into a silky sauce. Don't let it scramble!
