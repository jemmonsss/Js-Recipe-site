ingredients:
  - 250g pizza dough
  - 150g crushed tomatoes
  - 125g fresh mozzarella
  - 8 basil leaves
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/4 tsp salt
steps:
  - Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F) with a stone or tray inside.
  - Stretch the dough into a thin round.
  - Spread tomato, season with salt, and dot with torn mozzarella.
  - Bake 10–12 minutes until the crust is blistered.
  - Top with basil and olive oil before serving.
---

For a crispier base, bake on a preheated pizza stone and use the highest oven setting you have.
