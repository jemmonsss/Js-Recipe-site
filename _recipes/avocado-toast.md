ingredients:
  - 2 slices sourdough bread
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 1/2 lemon
  - 1 pinch chili flakes
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1/4 tsp salt
steps:
  - Toast the sourdough slices until golden.
  - Mash the avocado with lemon juice and salt.
  - Spread onto the toast and drizzle with olive oil.
  - Sprinkle with chili flakes and serve.
---

Use a very ripe avocado for the creamiest result. Add a poached egg on top for extra protein.
