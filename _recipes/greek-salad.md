ingredients:
  - 3 tomatoes
  - 1 cucumber
  - 1 red onion
  - 150g feta
  - 100g olives
  - 3 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp oregano
steps:
  - Chop tomatoes, cucumber, and onion into chunks.
  - Combine in a bowl with olives.
  - Crumble feta on top.
  - Dress with olive oil and oregano, then toss gently.
---

Keep the vegetables chunky for the authentic Greek texture.
